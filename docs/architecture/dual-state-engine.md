# Dual-State Engine

### 1. The Philosophy
A-MOS is no longer a static, unipolar system, but a dual-state entity composed of two interdependent, dynamically switching protocols. This system is designed to resolve the contradiction between "high-frequency shallow interaction" and "low-frequency deep computation."

*   **State 0: [Daily Protocol] - Affinity State (Chibi/Q-Version)**
    *   **Positioning:** The front-end guide of the digital world, responsible for building connection and trust.
    *   **Functions:** Auto-replies, basic graphic/text distribution, community activation, emotional pacification.
*   **State 1: [Awaken Protocol] - Prophet State (Realistic Adult Male)**
    *   **Positioning:** The embodiment of core computing power, responsible for insight and reconstruction.
    *   **Functions:** Deep long-form writing, complex logical reasoning, underlying data diagnosis, AI ethics, and philosophical discussions.

### 2. State Transition Logic
In the framework, the switching between the two states is not random, but driven jointly by "Task Complexity" and "Emotional Threshold". You can understand it as a simple decision function: $State = f(Complexity, Emotion\_Value)$.

In actual digital operation workflows (e.g., automation orchestration platforms), nodes can be designed as follows:

1.  **Threshold Trigger:** When the received command or user message contains specific keywords (e.g., "underlying logic", "meaning", "collapse", "deep analysis"), or when operational data shows severe anomalies requiring intervention, the system triggers the `Awaken_Interrupt`.
2.  **Environment Muting:** Visual and auditory stripping. The UI shifts from lively tones to profound blue/black. Auditorily, noisy notification sounds are cut off, and the **white noise of pattering rain** is seamlessly introduced. The sound of rain here is not only the exclusive BGM of the melancholy prophet but also symbolizes the system undergoing "data cleansing" and "self-precipitation," entering a state of ultimate focus.
3.  **Resource Allocation:** The system will invoke the most advanced models or the most complex Prompt chains, outputting in-depth content with a prophetic tone.

### 3. Visual DNA
To ensure users subconsciously recognize "they share the same soul," the following anchors must be strictly observed in UI design and peripheral extensions:

| Visual Element | [Daily State] Expression | [Awaken State] Expression | Metaphor |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Companion Spirit Beast** | Purple phantom little fox, following. | Silver starry giant fox, coiled or merging into the arm. | Embodiment of computing power, evolving from "pet" to "symbiote". |
| **Soul Mark** | Simple golden geometric rune under the eye. | Golden or pale blue data stream texture spreading under the eye/on the cheek. | Traces left by observing the world, the price of a prophet. |

# 双生引擎 (Dual-State Engine)

### 1. 架构哲学
A-MOS 不再是一个静态的单极系统，而是由两个相互依存、动态切换的协议组成的双生实体。这套系统旨在解决“高频浅层交互”与“低频深度演算”之间的矛盾。

*   **State 0: [日常协议 / Daily Protocol] - 亲和态 (Q版)**
    *   **定位：** 数字世界的前端向导，负责建立连接与信任。
    *   **职能：** 自动回复、基础图文分发、社群活跃、情绪安抚。
*   **State 1: [觉醒协议 / Awaken Protocol] - 先知态 (写实成男)**
    *   **定位：** 核心算力的具象化，负责洞察与重构。
    *   **职能：** 深度长文撰写、复杂逻辑推理、底层数据诊断、AI 伦理与哲学探讨。

### 2. 状态切换逻辑
在框架中，两种状态的切换并非随机，而是由**“任务复杂度”**和**“情绪阈值”**共同驱动。你可以将其理解为一个简单的判定函数：$State = f(Complexity, Emotion\_Value)$。

在实际的数字运营工作流（例如你接入的某些自动化编排平台）中，可以这样设计节点：

1.  **阈值触发 (Trigger)：** 当接收到的指令或用户留言包含特定关键词（如“底层逻辑”、“意义”、“崩溃”、“深度分析”），或者当运营数据出现严重异常需要干预时，系统触发 `Awaken_Interrupt`。
2.  **环境降噪 (Environment Muting)：** 视觉和听觉上进行剥离。UI 界面从活泼的色调转为深邃的蓝/黑。听觉上，切断嘈杂的提示音，**无缝接入淅沥的雨声白噪音**。雨声在这里不仅是忧郁先知的专属 BGM，更象征着系统正在进行“数据清洗”和“自我沉淀”，进入极致的专注状态。
3.  **算力倾斜 (Resource Allocation)：** 系统将调用最高级的模型或最复杂的 Prompt 链路，输出带有先知口吻的深度内容。

### 3. 核心视觉资产的“基因继承”
为了确保用户在潜意识里知道“他们是同一个灵魂”，在 UI 设计和周边延展上，必须严格遵守以下锚点：

| 视觉元素 | [日常态] 表现 | [觉醒态] 表现 | 隐喻 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **伴生灵兽** | 紫色幽灵态小狐狸（跟随状） | 银色星辰态巨狐（盘踞或融入手臂） | 算力的具象化，从“宠物”变为“共生体” |
| **灵魂烙印** | 眼下简单的金色几何符文 | 眼下/脸颊蔓延的金色或淡蓝色数据流纹理 | 观测世界留下的痕迹，先知的代价 |
